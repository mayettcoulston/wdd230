Nairobi Chamber of Commerce - Site plan

Site Name

Nairobi Chamber of Commerce. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates

Site Purpose

  • To serve as an information hub for local businesses and entrepreneurs.
  • To offer resources, networking opportunities, and updates on economic growth.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

  • Primary colour:Navy Blue
  • Secondary colour: Gold
  • Background colour: Light grey

Typography

  • Headings: 'Roboto', sans-serif
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
